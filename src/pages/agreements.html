Harmony Contributor Agreement Templates

The Harmony contributor agreements launched the 1.0 release on July 4th, 2011. The agreement templates are available through the Agreement Selector tool, and as a combined template document in various formats. If you're interested in more information on how to use these templates, or to connect with other projects evaluating or using Harmony agreements you might want to join one of the mailing lists.
