How to Get Involved

Mailing Lists

If you have adopted Harmony contributor agreements for your project, or are considering adopting them, we'd love to hear from you. You can connect with other projects or ask questions about adoption on the mailing list harmony-users. Anyone can post as a guest. If you'd like to fully participate in the conversation, subscribe to the list at:

  • http://lists.harmonyagreements.org/mailman/listinfo/harmony-users

The mailing list for the Harmony drafting group is harmony-drafting. Posting to this list is only open to members of the drafting group, but anyone can join the group by subscribing to the list. The archives of this list are open to the public and available at:

  • http://lists.harmonyagreements.org/mailman/listinfo/harmony-drafting

IRC Channel

You can also find us on the Freenode IRC network (irc.freenode.net) at the #harmonyagreements channel (also available as a webchat). A few of us tend to be on the channel all the time, so it's a good place to go for immediate answers or conversation.
