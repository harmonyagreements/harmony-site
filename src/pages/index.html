What is HARMONY?

Project Harmony is a community-centered group focused on free software contribution agreements, launched in May 2010 by Amanda Brock, General Counsel for Canonical. As a group, we represent a diverse collection of perspectives, experiences, communities, projects, non-profit and for-profit entities. In that diversity, we share a common belief in the future of free software, and a common interest in using our skills (whether they're legal, organizational, editorial, technical, or otherwise) to the benefit of software freedom...
