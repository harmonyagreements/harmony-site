How to Get Involved

Mailing Lists

The mailing list for public review of the Harmony contributor agreement drafts is harmony-review. Anyone can post as a guest. If you'd like to fully participate in the conversation, subscribe to the list at:

  • http://lists.harmonyagreements.org/mailman/listinfo/harmony-review

The mailing list for the Harmony drafting group is harmony-drafting. Posting to this list is only open to members of the drafting group, but anyone can join the group by subscribing to the list. The archives of this list are open to the public and available at:

  • http://lists.harmonyagreements.org/mailman/listinfo/harmony-drafting

IRC Channel

You can also find us on the Freenode IRC network (irc.freenode.net) at the #harmonyagreements channel (also available as a webchat). A few of us tend to be on the channel all the time, so it's a good place to go for immediate answers or conversation.

Review Process

The Alpha review phase of the Harmony contributor agreements is now closed. We are currently integrating all comments into new drafts for the next meeting of the Harmony drafting group on May 18th, 2011. Please continue to contribute all comments, suggestions, corrections, and discussions on the harmony-review mailing list.

In addition to general discussion, the mailing list traffic also feeds into the comment review page. We use a special format to automatically extract comments from the mailing list messages. To mark off a comment in your email message, just put ==== before and after each comment, and note the line numbers. For example, this comment in an email message:

====
lines 6-8

This is an example comment.
====

would display as a comment next to line 6 in the comment review page. Please take the line numbers for your comments from that page, and not from the PDF or ODF documents.
